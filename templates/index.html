How it works

Three steps to start
exchanging skills

No payments, no subscriptions. Just your time and skills.

01 — Register
🙋
Create your profile
Sign up and list the skills you can teach. You start with 10 free time credits. Upload proof or take a skill test to get ⭐ Verified status and rank higher in matches.
02 — Request
🔎
Request a skill
Need to learn something? Submit a request. Our priority-queue matching algorithm pairs you with the most legitimate available provider — verified skills get matched first.
03 — Confirm
✅
Complete and confirm
Your credits are locked in escrow at match time. After the session, both parties confirm completion — credits release to the provider. Dispute? Credits are refunded instantly.
Why trust us
